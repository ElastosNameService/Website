FAQ

What is blockchain domains?
A blockchain domain is an asset on the blockchain. Blockchain domains are designed to resolve your domains and wallet using smart contracts.
Can I transfer my blockchain domains?
Yes, ELA domains are ERC721(NFT), you can transfer your domains with the wallets that supports ERC721.
Does ELA domain compatible with traditional ICANN domains?
No, ELA domains are built with blockchain technology.
